A Call To Action: Unsafe Practices

According to the World Health Organization, unsafe injection practices are responsible for transmitting an estimated 20 million cases of hepatitis B (HBV),two million cases of hepatitis C (HCV) and approximately 260 000 cases of HIV each year. In developing and transitional nations, unsafe injections account for 32% of new HBV infections.
